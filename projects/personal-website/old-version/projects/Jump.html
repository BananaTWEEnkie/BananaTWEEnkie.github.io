Jump

A game built in Unity and C#. You have lived all your life in the perfect place. One day, you awoke to many screams that you soon realize were your friends. Everywhere you looked, there was nothing but flames and the smoke that risen from it. You must escape to safety up on the trees where you left your hot air balloon. But your friends need your help! Can you save them all before the fire engulfs you?
